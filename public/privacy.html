Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

1. Data Collection

Grain is a local-first application. All SVG noise generation and texture synthesis are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data from your device.

2. No Server Processing

Unlike many online tools, Grain does not send your noise parameters, frequency values, or texture data to any server. All processing happens client-side, ensuring complete privacy.

3. No Cookies or Tracking

Grain does not use cookies, local storage, or any tracking technologies. We do not track your usage patterns or collect analytics data.

4. Third-Party Services

Grain does not use any third-party services for data processing. The application runs entirely on your device using standard web technologies.

5. Children's Privacy

Grain is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through Velocity Digital Architecture House at velocity.calyvent.com
